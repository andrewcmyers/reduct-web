The Reduct game is part of a research study that is collecting information and feedback about student interactions with an online game designed to teach computer programming concepts.

Privacy/Confidentiality/Data Security

Data from your play-through of the game will be anonymously logged and kept private. In any sort of public report we will not include any information that will make it possible to identify you.

Taking part is voluntary

Your participation in this study is voluntary, and you may play the game without participating in the study.

If you have other questions
